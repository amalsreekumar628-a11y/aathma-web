Who We Are

Built on precision, not improvisation

AATHMA began as a small decor and design practice and has grown into a full-service event management house over more than a decade. What hasn't changed is the discipline behind it — walking a venue twice before anyone else arrives, and treating every detail as load-bearing.

We don't run off a checklist. We read the room: how a family moves through a wedding day, where a corporate audience's attention drifts, what a venue needs to feel intentional rather than rented. Every event is planned around the people inside it, not a template.
